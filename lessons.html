Four Pillars of My Teaching

Jazz & Improvisation

Explore the language of jazz and develop your improvisational skills across multiple styles. From bebop to fusion, learn to express yourself musically with confidence and creativity.

Applied Music Theory

Understand the building blocks of music in a practical, hands-on way. Theory becomes powerful when you apply it directly to your guitar and compositions.

Digital Studio & DAW Mastery

Learn professional music production using Ableton Live and other DAWs. From recording and mixing to sound design, develop skills to create professional-quality music at home.

Versatile Musicianship

Build a strong foundation in jazz while exploring blues, fusion, classical, and modern styles. Focus on tone, touch, musicality, and making thoughtful musical decisions in any genre.

My Philosophy as a Guitarist & Instructor

Music is not just about playing the right notes—it's about understanding the "why" behind those notes and expressing your unique voice as a musician. My approach combines technical excellence with creative freedom, grounded in a deep understanding of music theory and its real-world applications.

I believe every student, regardless of age or experience level, has the potential to become a confident, versatile musician. My years working with students of all ages have taught me how to adapt my teaching style to match each person's learning pace and musical goals. Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced player looking to expand your horizons, I'm here to guide you on your musical journey.

In my lessons, we focus on building skills that last—critical listening, problem-solving, and musical intuition. You'll learn not just how to play, but how to think like a musician and make informed, creative decisions in any musical situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long are lessons?

Standard lessons are 30, 45, or 60 minutes, depending on your preference and goals. We can discuss what works best during your free consultation.

Can I take lessons online?

Absolutely! Online lessons via Discord work just as well as in-person. You'll need a stable internet connection and your guitar, and we can get started from anywhere.

Do you teach only jazz?

While jazz is my foundation, I teach a wide range of styles including blues, fusion, classic rock, and modern music. We'll focus on what interests you most.

Are you open to students with no experience?

Yes! I work with complete beginners of all ages. We'll start at your level and progress at a pace that feels comfortable for you.

How do I know if I'm ready for lessons?

Everyone is ready! Whether you've never touched a guitar or been playing for years, a free consultation will help us determine the best starting point for your journey.
